Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a service request through our website, we collect the following information:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Service address
  • Description of your HVAC issue or service request
  • Preferred date and time for service

We also collect standard analytics data (pages visited, browser type, referral source) through Google Analytics to improve our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Contact you to confirm your service appointment
  • Provide the HVAC services you requested
  • Send you service reminders and follow-up communications
  • Improve our website and customer experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Service request data is transmitted to our Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system via a secure HTTPS connection. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Analytics for anonymous traffic analysis. Google Analytics collects data subject to Google’s Privacy Policy. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics.

5. Cookies

We use cookies only for Google Analytics. These are anonymous session and preference cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our website or request service.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of future marketing communications
