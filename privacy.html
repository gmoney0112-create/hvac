SMS messaging

By providing your phone number and submitting an estimate request, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Apex Tile & Remodeling. These messages may include:

  • Estimate confirmations and scheduling updates

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any SMS message at any time. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP to any SMS message or contact us at info@apextileremodeling.com.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information — including your phone number or SMS consent — to any third parties.
