Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project — from estimates and permits to timelines, materials, and our 5-year warranty.

Estimates & Pricing
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes — absolutely. We provide free on-site consultations for all projects. We visit your property, take measurements, discuss your vision, and deliver a detailed written quote within 24 hours. No pressure, no obligation.
How accurate are your estimates?
Very accurate. Because we measure on-site and itemize labor and materials separately, our quotes rarely change. If anything unexpected arises mid-project, we discuss it with you before proceeding. No surprise invoices.
What is your payment schedule?
Typically a deposit is collected at contract signing (usually 30–40% for materials), with progress payments tied to project milestones, and a final payment upon completion. Exact terms are outlined in your written contract.
Can I supply my own materials?
Yes. We’re happy to install customer-supplied tile, flooring, or fixtures. Keep in mind our 5-year workmanship warranty covers installation labor only — not defects in materials we did not supply. We’ll always confirm material specs before starting.
Project Timelines
How long does a bathroom remodel typically take?
A full bathroom gut-and-rebuild typically takes 2–4 weeks depending on scope, tile complexity, and fixture lead times. A targeted upgrade (tile only, vanity swap) can be completed in 3–7 business days. We provide a project schedule at contract signing.
How long does a kitchen remodel take?
Kitchen remodels range from 3–8 weeks depending on whether cabinets are being replaced, countertop materials selected, and permit requirements. We stage the work to minimize disruption to your household.
How do you handle project timelines and scheduling?
We build a detailed project schedule before work begins and share it with you. We staff projects so they run continuously — no starting and stopping. If anything affects the timeline, we communicate immediately and adjust the schedule together.
Permits & Compliance
Do you handle permits?
Yes. For projects that require permits (structural work, additions, certain plumbing or electrical changes), we pull and manage all permits on your behalf as your licensed contractor. Permit fees are included in your quoted price.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. Apex Tile & Remodeling holds an active Texas contractor’s license and carries full general liability insurance and workers’ compensation. We can provide certificate of insurance on request.
Materials & Design
What tile brands and suppliers do you work with?
We source from a wide network of suppliers including Dal-Tile, Florida Tile, MSI, and local San Antonio distributors. We can accommodate custom tile orders, imported stone, and large-format porcelain. If you have a specific product in mind, we can usually source it.
Do you offer design consultations?
Yes. During your free estimate visit, we discuss tile patterns, material options, and finishes. For larger projects, we can connect you with a design partner or work from your own design plans, Pinterest boards, or inspiration photos.
Can you match existing tile or flooring for a repair?
We do our best to source matching materials for repairs and partial replacements. Bring us a sample or a photo and we’ll search our supplier network. For discontinued tile, we may suggest a complementary solution and walk you through the options.
Warranty & After-Project
What is included in your 5-year warranty?
Our 5-year workmanship warranty covers all installation labor: tile adhesion and grouting, floor and wall installation, waterproofing, and structural integrity of our work. If anything we installed fails due to workmanship within 5 years, we return and repair it at no cost to you.
How do I maintain my new tile?
Use a pH-neutral cleaner on all tile surfaces. Avoid harsh acids or bleach on natural stone. We recommend sealing grout lines annually in high-moisture areas like showers. We provide a care guide for every completed project with product recommendations specific to your tile type.
What if I notice an issue after the project is complete?
Contact us immediately. We perform a final walk-through with every client before marking a project complete, but if something surfaces afterward, we’re a phone call away. Our 5-year warranty means you’re covered, and we stand behind our work unconditionally.
Commercial Projects
Can you do commercial projects?
Yes. We handle commercial tile, flooring, and finish work for restaurants, retail spaces, offices, and mixed-use builds. Commercial projects receive a dedicated project manager and a schedule built around your occupancy requirements. Contact us for a custom quote.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the greater San Antonio area including Stone Oak, Alamo Heights, Shavano Park, Helotes, the Northwest Side, the Medical Center district, Downtown SA, and all surrounding communities. Call us if you’re unsure whether we cover your area — we usually do.
